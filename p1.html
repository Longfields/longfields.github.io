About

swords What is Clash of Code?

Clash of Code was founded in 2021 by CowboysFan232. That same year, Liam K. became the first champion. Every year Clash of Code creates some of the best programs on the hotlist, and we are proud to be in our fourth year still going strong.

This is a collaborative challenge where participants are tasked with teaming up to create awesome programs. See the full schedule here.

Groups_3 How do I get drafted?

Rather than signing up for individual teams, participants will sign up in a joining thread, and then be drafted onto a team by one of our amazing captains. While you may not get to choose exactly which team you want, this way we can ensure fairness across the teams.

Due to the fact that this is a collaborative competition, not everyone will be placed on a team. Limited spots are available. However, this is primarily to ensure that only active coders will be chosen. If there is a lot of interest, we have a plan to expand the number of people able to join.
